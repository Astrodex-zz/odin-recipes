Pirozhki

  • 1 1/3 cups of milk
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 table spoon of sugar
  • 3 1/2 cups of plain flour
  • 2 teaspoons of yeast

Warm up milk in the microwave oven to ~35 degrees, mix salt, sugar, yeast and 1 cup of flour into the milk. Let it sit in the warm place until the mixture raises, mix in the rest of the flour. The dough should be fairly soft, but not too soft, or it will tear when you fold pirogi.

Fold into shape with filling, then fry in oil

FILLING

brown 1 large onion in a frying pan, add 300g of mince (best to mix 150g each of beef and lamb mince), fry until cooked. Make mashed potatoes from 4 large potatoes, mix in the mince/onion mixture, salt and pepper to taste.
